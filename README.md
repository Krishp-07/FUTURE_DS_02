 # 📊 Customer Retention & Churn Analysis Dashboard

A comprehensive **Customer Retention & Churn Analysis Dashboard** built using **Power BI** to analyze customer behavior, identify churn patterns, and provide actionable business recommendations for improving customer retention.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the most important metrics for subscription-based businesses. This project analyzes customer data from a telecom company to understand:

- Why customers leave the service
- Which customer segments have the highest churn
- Customer retention trends
- Business strategies to reduce churn and increase customer lifetime value

The dashboard is designed to simulate a real-world business intelligence report that can be presented to product managers, business stakeholders, or startup founders.

# 📌 Key Insights

## 1. Overall Churn

- Total Customers: **7,043**
- Churned Customers: **1,869**
- Active Customers: **5,174**
- Overall Churn Rate: **26.54%**

Approximately **1 in every 4 customers** leaves the company.

---

## 2. Contract Type

Customers with **Month-to-Month contracts** have the highest churn.

Customers with **One-Year** and **Two-Year contracts** are significantly more loyal.

### Business Recommendation

Encourage customers to switch from Month-to-Month plans to longer contracts by offering:

- Discounts
- Loyalty rewards
- Exclusive benefits

---

## 3. Internet Service

Customers using **Fiber Optic Internet** have the highest churn.

Customers without internet service show the lowest churn.

### Business Recommendation

Investigate:

- Service quality
- Pricing
- Customer support

for Fiber Optic customers.

---

## 4. Gender Analysis

Male and Female customers exhibit nearly identical churn behavior.

### Business Recommendation

Gender should not be considered a significant predictor of churn.

Focus on behavioral and subscription-related factors instead.

---

## 5. Customer Tenure

Customers within their **first year (0–12 months)** have the highest churn rate.

Churn decreases steadily as customer tenure increases.

### Business Recommendation

Improve onboarding experience by:

- Welcome emails
- Product tutorials
- Personalized support
- Early engagement campaigns

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following strategies can reduce churn:

- Introduce incentives for long-term contracts.
- Improve Fiber Optic customer experience.
- Strengthen onboarding during the first 12 months.
- Launch proactive retention campaigns for high-risk customers.
- Reward loyal customers with exclusive offers.
- Monitor churn KPIs regularly using the dashboard.
